About HealthWatch

HealthWatch is a start-up smart monitoring health service. The company develops technology that monitors the vital signs of

its clients in a home setting and generates alerts and other data based on the monitored vital signs. It monitors vital health

signs such as heart rate and the ability to detect a fall through a worn smart bracelet. The goal of the company is to allow

individuals with health concerns to live a more independent life through non-intrusive technology methods.
